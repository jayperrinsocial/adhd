ADHD A–Z Glossary

Clear, easy-to-understand definitions of ADHD-related terms.

A – Attention

The ability to focus on a task or activity without being easily distracted.

B – Burnout

A state of emotional, mental, and physical exhaustion often linked to prolonged stress or masking.

C – Co-occurring Conditions

Other mental health or neurodevelopmental conditions that can occur alongside ADHD.
