ADHD A–Z Glossary

Explore clear, easy-to-understand definitions of ADHD-related terms.

ABCDE FGHIJ KLMNO PQRST UVWXYZ

A

Attention: The ability to focus on a task or activity without being easily distracted.

ADHD: Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder — a neurodevelopmental condition that affects attention, impulsivity, and activity levels.

B

Burnout: A state of emotional, mental, and physical exhaustion often linked to prolonged stress or masking.

C

Co-occurring Conditions: Other mental health or neurodevelopmental conditions that can occur alongside ADHD, such as anxiety or dyslexia.

Comorbidity: The presence of two or more conditions in one person at the same time.

T

Time Blindness: Difficulty perceiving and managing time effectively, common in ADHD.

Z

Zoom Fatigue: Feeling mentally drained after long video meetings or online interactions — can impact attention and energy.
