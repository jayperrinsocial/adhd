Challenges of ADHD

Understanding the common struggles faced by people with ADHD — and finding ways to overcome them.

Living with ADHD can be tough, especially when it comes to organisation, focus, relationships, and self-esteem. Recognising these challenges and learning practical coping strategies can help reduce frustration and make daily life smoother.
