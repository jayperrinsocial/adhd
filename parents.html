Children in Education

Guidance for parents navigating ADHD in the education system — from early support to SEND plans and beyond.

Understanding ADHD within an educational environment can be overwhelming for both parents and children. From communicating with teachers to accessing additional support, the right approach can make a huge difference in a child’s confidence, learning, and wellbeing. Below are common questions and guidance to help you advocate effectively for your child.

  • 7) What if my school refuses support or assessment?
  • 8) How can I help my child manage homework and focus?
  • 9) What training do teachers receive about ADHD?
