The Science

Understanding the brain, neurology, and research behind ADHD.

ADHD is a complex neurodevelopmental condition that affects attention, motivation, and executive function. The science behind it continues to evolve — from brain imaging studies to genetic research — helping us understand why ADHD presents differently in each individual.
