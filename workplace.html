Workplace & Career

Guidance on navigating ADHD in the workplace — understanding your rights, managing focus, and thriving in your career.

ADHD can affect productivity, communication, and organisation at work, but it also brings unique strengths such as creativity, problem-solving, and high energy. Knowing your rights, understanding reasonable adjustments, and building strategies can help you perform at your best in any environment.
